The Red Cross Youth (RCY) of Calamba Doctors' College is a vibrant and compassionate student-led organization affiliated with the Philippine Red Cross. It aims to empower young individuals to take active roles in first aid, outreach programs, health promotion, and disaster response. Through its initiatives, CDC-RCY promotes volunteerism and service with a heart—within the school and the wider community.
