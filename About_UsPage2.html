Spearheaded by the initiative from Reginald Palines (Chairman), who initially worked as a volunteer, the organization was built upon the idea of volunteerism as the main objective in mind. He, at first, dreamt of it as a group who is ready to respond during calamities. But now, it is able to host its own mobile blood donation drives, which he believes is the most beneficial way to give help to others.
The organization was credited and recognized by the school in October of 2023.
